ALPR (Automatic License Plate Reader) Transparency Project in Harrisburg PA

Right-to-Know Request

This site publishes records received through a Pennsylvania Right-to-Know (RTK) request filed with the City of Harrisburg.

The City was professional to work with and responded on time with the requested materials. The documents shared here are representative examples of the records received.

Example Documents

Below are example documents illustrating the types of records provided in response to the RTK request.
